Privacy Policy

ScanPlanHVAC for iOS

Effective date: March 22, 2026

ScanPlanHVAC ("the App") is an HVAC load calculation and floor plan tool that uses your device's LiDAR sensor to scan rooms, generate measurements, and produce reports. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

The App collects and processes the following types of data, all of which are generated by you during normal use:

  • Room scan data — 3D point clouds, meshes, and measurements captured by the device's LiDAR sensor and ARKit framework.
  • Project information — Project names, room names, building envelope details, and other values you enter manually.
  • Device orientation — Compass heading data from CoreLocation, used solely to determine real-world wall orientations (N/S/E/W).

2. How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device. The App does not currently transmit any data to external servers, cloud services, or third parties. Your scan data, projects, and reports remain entirely on your iPhone or iPad.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not currently integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

In a future version, the App may offer optional cloud storage and syncing powered by Google Firebase. If and when this feature is introduced, this privacy policy will be updated to reflect what data is transmitted, how it is stored, and how you can manage or delete it. Cloud features will always be opt-in.

4. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera & LiDAR — Required to scan rooms and capture 3D measurements.
  • Location (compass only) — Used to determine compass heading for wall orientation. The App does not track or store your geographic location.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete individual projects or rooms within the App. Uninstalling the App will remove all associated data from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
